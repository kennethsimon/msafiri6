Mobile safari

Msafiri Mobile Camps beckons you to exceptional African hospitality and experiences. The true African hospitality is not lost in the wilderness. We ensure that your every need, comfort and privacy is given the utmost priority. The campsites are chosen for their location, providing beautiful views, cool breezes, total privacy and proximity to wildlife. The camp crew departs a few days before your arrival to set up camp and other facilities needed. Bottled water is used for drinking, coffees, tea and cleaning vegetables and fruits.

Accomodation

The tents are furnished with king-size double or twin beds, which are made daily with crispy clean linen laid over a thick, comfortable mattress. Mosquito netting on all doors and windows provides security from insects whilst allowing the cooling breezes to circulate inside the tent. A bedside table, reading lamp and torch complete the simple but tasteful furnishings. Each tent has an en-suite bathroom appointed with shaving mirrors, washstand, soft towels, soap and shampoos.

Meals

Be awoken in the morning by the smell of freshly baked bread coming from our bush oven!!! Our chef will not only spoil you with freshly made food, freshly squeezed fruit juices but will also take your taste buds on a wild culinary safari. Your menu will include different cuisines from Africa, Asia and continental Europe all served with a selection of wonderful wines. A wide variety of drinks are on offer in the well stocked bar. Our experienced bar tender will be delighted to mix your favourite cocktail together but would be just as happy preparing refreshing ice-cold filtered water with a twist of lemon.

Activities

Various activities are conducted from the camps: boat safaris, walking safaris and game drives. Our camps are the bases from which you can enjoy your adventure. Depending on the location our guest can appreciate each place by boat, by vehicle or on foot, with each experience offering a varied perspective and sense stimulation.
