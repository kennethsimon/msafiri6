Camping safari

Accommodation is in two persons tents equipped with thick mattresses, bed sheets, blankets, pillows and all other necessary camping gear. On these non - participatory safaris, the cook will be in charge of cooking, cleaning and the setting up tents. For those on a budget but do not want to camp, guesthouses or bandas are an alternative option.
