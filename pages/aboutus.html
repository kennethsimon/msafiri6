ABOUT US

As it is well known, safaris started in East Africa. When the early settlers went on a safari, they packed all that was needed: tents, chairs, cutlery, food, drinks, crystal glasses off with no luxury possible left behind. The safari crew always went a few days ahead to set up camp – and these are the origins of safaris and in particular the Mobile Camping safaris.

This is the experience that Msafiri Travels wants to share with you. We know that a personal touch adds greatly to your enjoyment and the memories of your holidays. We, therefore, carefully select an itinerary and safari programme that is carved around your personal requirements, complemented by experienced, talented and skilful personnel to fully cater for your needs. We assure you of our professionalism, attentiveness, promptness, creativity, punctuality and that very personalised touch.

We have inspected each hotel, camp or lodge that is features on our website or itineraries thus you are receiving firsthand information with no detail left out.

Whether you are a solo traveller, a family or looking to join a Group Safari, there is a safari for everyone at pocket-friendly prices without compromising quality.

Our Camping safaris are in two-person tents (3 x3m) equipped with mattresses, bed sheets, blankets, pillows and all other necessary camping gear. On these in participatory safaris, you will be accompanied by a cook and guide who will be in charge of all chores. Camping will either be in public/communal or private camps depending on requirements and budget.

Are you seeking to explore Africa like the early explorers and settlers did but still seek some comfort or luxury and the smell of freshly baked bread? Our Mobile Camps will give you the greatest freedom to explore the African wilderness. With a maximum of 10 people per camp, these semi-luxury and luxury tents are suitable for a group of friends, honeymooners or families. Each camp has a crew of: cooks, waiters, camp attendants, guides and a host/ess.

If comfort and luxury is your thing, our Lodge safaris offer various levels of accommodation from the middle-level comfortable lodges, to the totally exclusive luxurious lodges for the discerning traveller.

Our multi-destination cross boarder safaris combining any of the five countries, Tanzania. , Malawi, Zambia and Zimbabwe, road and/or flight in private or small group safaris.

A safari is not complete without a few days of relaxation on the Coast of East Africa. Whether it’s Unguja (famously known as Zanzibar), Pemba in Tanzania, Pemba in Northern Mozambique or the Lake Shores of Lake Malawi, these beaches are untouched with a variety of accommodation to choose from.

Our services are not confined to safaris alone. Contact us for: accommodation and flight reservations, airport transfers, car hire, conferences, cross border road transfers, excursions, incentive travel, family safaris, team building, Mountain climbing, photographic and incentives travels.
