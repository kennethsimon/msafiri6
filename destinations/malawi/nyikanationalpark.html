Nyika National Park

In the far north of Malawi, at an altitude of around 2,500 m the Nyika National Park protects over 3,000 sq km of this rich plateau, noted for the endemic herbs and heathers found in its rolling, montane grasslands. The wild, open nature of the plateau attracts visitors who come to view the birds and animals, study the flowers, walk or ride across the hills and valleys, or simply sit in the sun and absorb the magnificent scenery. Large herds of roan, reedbuck, zebra and eland gather here, together with leopard, serval and spotted hyena.
