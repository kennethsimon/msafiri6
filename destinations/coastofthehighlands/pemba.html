Pemba

Pemba is mysterious, magical and enigmatic with beautiful landscape and beaches. Remote and receiving very few travellers it offers spectacular diving locations, characterised by clear blue waters. Pemba is also famous for traditional bull fighting, witchcraft and cloves.
