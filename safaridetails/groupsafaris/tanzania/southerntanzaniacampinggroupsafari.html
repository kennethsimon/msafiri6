10 DAYS SOUTHERN GROUP CAMPING SAFARI

Low season offer book 4 people and get 15% discount
03 June - 23rd March 2019
  • Day to Day
  • Details
  • Location
  • Day 1 - Dar es salaam To Udzungwa

    After breakfast and safari briefing, depart from Dar es Salaam at 07.30 for Udzungwa National Park. On arrival and time permitting, short walk in the park and overnight at Campsite, full board

  • Day 2 - Sanje waterfalls, Udzungwa National Park

    After breakfast take a walking in the park and thereafter go for up the Sanje waterfalls for a refreshing swim and stunning views. Overnight at Udzungwa Hondo Hondo Tented Camp, full boardUDZUNGWA NATIONAL PARK opened to the public 1992 and one of the most beautiful wilderness areas in Tanzania. Covering an area of 1900 sq km., the uniqueness of this park lies in its biodiversity in hosting a variety of animals and plant species not found anywhere else in the world. The red Colobus and the Sane crested manbrey, elephants, buffalos, leopards, and sable antelopes are some of the residents in this park. A good range of birdlife is present which includes Rufus winged sunbird, Iringa akalat, Lagden’s bush shrike and Udzungwa partridge.

  • Day 3 - To Ruaha National Park

    Depart in the morning after breakfast for Ruaha National Park. The drive to Ruaha is a truly fascinating one which gives you the real feel of the African countryside. The journey will see you pass sisal plantations, local villages before climbing the Great Rift Escarpment passing through Iringa Town and finally arriving to your Lodge. Arrival time will be late afternoon and overnight at Ruaha Hilltop Lodge, for the night, full board.

  • Day 4 - Ruaha National Park

    Today you will enter the Ruaha National Park in search of game life. Leaving the lodge immediately after breakfast, we arrive at the parks’ gate search of the elusive wild dogs, herds of elephants and buffaloes. After picnic lunch, continue with late afternoon/evening game drive before departing for the lodge. This is the time that predators begin their hunt for their evening meal, dinner and over night at Ruaha Hilltop Lodge, full board.

  • Day 5 - Walking Safari in Ruaha National Park

    After breakfast, depart for a walking safari which will bring you close to some of the smallest animals and plants that you would not see on a vehicle game drive. The crisp fresh African air mixed with the wild smell of the animals evokes your smell sense to the intoxication unique bush smell of Africa. Each plant and tree has medicinal value in the African Community and our guide will point them out for you. Game drive follows and overnight at Ruaha Hilltop Lodge, full board.

  • Day 6-Depart for Mikumi National Park

    After breakfast depart for Mikumi National Park via a traditional African village at Twingamalenga. Late afternoon game drive and overnight at Mikumi Safari Lodge, full board. MIKUMI NATIONAL PARK is flanked between the Uluguru and Udzungwa Mountains. Mikumi National Park is home to buffalos, giraffes, elephants, lions, zebras and leopards. The park is also famous for its wild dogs and 6m long pythons.

  • Day 7 - Game drive in Mikumi National Park

    Early morning game drive in Mikumi before departing for Selous northern gate. The drive will be through some of the most scenic parts of Southern Tanzania. Villages and the Uluguru Mountains dot along the way. You will make a short lunch break by the road side arriving at the Camp by late afternoon. Late afternoon game drive and overnight at Selous Kinga Lodge, full board.

  • Day 8 - Exploring selous

    Take the day exploring Selous as the famous explorers did in the yesterday years. Picnic lunch will be under shady trees with a relaxing visit to the hot springs Overnight at Selous Kinga Lodge, full board.

  • Day 9 - Boat safari on The Great Rufiji River in Selous game reserve

    Early morning walking safari, followed by the afternoon boat safari. The boat safari brings you close to the aquatic animals and birds. Overnight at Selous Kinga Lodge, full board. Return to camp late evening in time to freshen up and for dinner.

  • Day 10 - Back to Dar es salaam

    Depart from Selous at 08.00 to Dar es salaam.
