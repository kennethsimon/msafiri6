  • Day 1 - Depart for Mikumi National Park

    Pick up from your hotel at 0600 and depart for Mikumi National Park. This will be a five hours drive. Picnic lunch will be provided. Late afternoon game drive and overnight at Mikumi Bandas, Half board ( bed and lunch and dinner).

  • Day 2 - Game drive and depart for Dar es salaam

    MIKUMI NATIONAL PARK is flanked between the Uluguru and Udzungwa Mountains and is transected by the surfaced road between Dar es Salaam and Iringa making it one of the most accessible parks in the southern Circuit. It also shares its boundary in the extreme south, with the Selous Game Reserve, and the two make one ecosystem with some animals (Elephant, Buffaloes and Zebras) migrating to and from the northern part of the reserve into Mikumi National Park Other animals to be seen in the park included: buffalos, giraffes, elephants, lions, zebras and occasionally leopards.
