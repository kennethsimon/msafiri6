  • Day 1 - Depart for Pemba

    Depart for Dar es Salaam and on arrival, transfer to Emerald Bay Lodge, Full board.

  • Day 2 - Pemba Island Tour

    Pemba is well known for it cloves and other spices. The tour begins with the tour of the old Oil distillery for Cloves oil, Eucalyptus and lemon grass oils. Make your purchase of these exotic old and thereafter you will go to visit the community clove plantation. Ngwezi Forest the last of the equatorial forest in the Island. The Light house was built in 1900 and gives views across the border into Mombasa, Kenya. Return to the Hotel at 17.30 and overnight at Emerald Bay Lodge, Full board.

  • Day 3&4 - Leisure

    Days at leisure with optional diving excursion and overnight at Emerald Bay Lodge, full board.

  • Day 5 - Depart for Dar-es-salaam

    Transfer to the airport for your flight to Dar es Salaam to the airport for your flight back home,
