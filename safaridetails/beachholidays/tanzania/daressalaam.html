Low season offer book 4 people and get 15% discount

  • Day1 - Transfer from airport

    Pick up from airport and transfer to Hotel bed and breakfast.

  • Day2 - Walking safari at Pande Game Reserve

    Depart at 06.30 for a 40 minutes drive to Pande Game Reserve. With an armed ranger, you will go for a 2-3 hours walking safari, exploring this game within the city outskirts. Expect to see bushbuck, birds, baboons, butterflies, blue balled moneys, and stunning views form the reserve. After a well deserved healthy breakfast, you will continue on to visit the nearby fishing villages as you return to the hotel in time for lunch. Afternoon at leisure and overnight at hotel, bed and breakfast

  • Day3 - Dar-es-salaam city tour

    After breakfast depart for a half day tour of Dar es Salaam. Visit the museum, Artisansâ€™ market and the waterfront. Return to the hotel at 13..00, transfer by boat to the nearby Mbudya Island for swimming, snorkeling or simply soaking in the sun. Return to the main land and overnight at hotel, bed and breakfast.

  • Day4 - Half day city tour in Dar-es-salaam

    Half day city tour which will include natural museam, botanical gardens, fish market ferry port, walking on the streets of Dar es Salaam. Return to the hotel and 14.00 and overnight at hotel, bed and breakfast.

    Dar es Salaam, meaning The Haven of Peace in Arabic, has one of the best deep natural harbours in the world. With a population of over 3,500,000 dwellers representing over 120 tribes of Tanzania with their different cultural backgrounds coming together has grown be to one of the most cosmopolitan cities in East Africa. A city tour of Dar es Salaam will take you to the National Museum, Nyerere Cultural Centre, the Village Museum, Mwenge Carvings Market, and the Magogoni Fish Market, among other interesting spots.

    Transfer to the airport for your flight back home
